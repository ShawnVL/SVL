Shawn Van Lith

Quality Assurance for JUNO Live Inc.

Shawn was the second member to join the Quality Assurance team and has been with JUNO since March of 2021. As a member of the QA team his focus is in helping to maintain the highest level of standards, as an individual as well as a team member, when it comes to assuring the best possible product is delivered to our clients.

While working closely with the rest of QA, Shawn helps to evaluate JUNO software with the intent to determine whether it is bug free, as well as identifying any visual or functional adjustments that may be needed. Other areas of focus include, database snapshots, Opentok setup, config testing, and identifying functions and features that may help make the JUNO experience as seamless and user friendly as possible.
